International Competition Options for the Saudi Youth Robotics Cohort (Ages 8–12)

Strategic alternatives to ITEX/WYIE for the 2026 cohort

Prepared on 15 April 2026

This report revises and strengthens the original recommendation framework so it more fully matches the requested decision structure: clear recommendation hierarchy, stronger comparison logic, more complete timing analysis, and a fuller elimination appendix for stakeholder review.

Executive Summary

The central question is not merely which event is prestigious, but which event offers the best combination of international reputation, age fit, domain alignment, and realistic timing for a Saudi youth robotics cohort now preparing after ITEX/WYIE timing has effectively become too near.

After reviewing leading global robotics and AI youth competitions, the strongest answer remains: prioritize WRO Future Innovators, maintain IRO 2026 as a serious secondary path subject to rule confirmation, and treat Robotics for Good as a valuable but timing-constrained opportunity for this cycle.
